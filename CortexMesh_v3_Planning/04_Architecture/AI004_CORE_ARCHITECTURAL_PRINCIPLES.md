# AI004 - Core Architectural Principles

Status: PROPOSED

---

## Purpose

Define the fundamental architectural principles that govern CortexMesh independently of implementation, policy, or specific AI models.

These principles describe how the architecture should evolve and provide design constraints for future work.

---

## Proposed Principles

### AP-01 Governance Before Implementation

Architectural evolution shall occur within the governance framework established by the board.

---

### AP-02 Evidence Before Conclusion

Architectural decisions shall be supported by evidence rather than intuition or preference.

---

### AP-03 Independent Agent Reasoning

Agents should perform independent analysis before any consensus process begins.

---

### AP-04 Human Authority

Human decision-makers remain the final authority for governance decisions.

---

### AP-05 Explainability

Architectural decisions should remain understandable and auditable.

---

### AP-06 Separation of Concerns

Governance, architecture, and implementation remain distinct responsibilities.

---

### AP-07 Implementation Independence

Architectural principles should remain valid regardless of programming language, model provider, or deployment environment.

---

### AP-08 Evolution Through Evidence

Architectural refinement should occur through documented evidence and board review rather than ad hoc change.
