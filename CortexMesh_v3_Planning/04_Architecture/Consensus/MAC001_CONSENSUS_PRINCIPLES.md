# MAC001 - Consensus Principles

Status: PROPOSED

---

## Purpose

Define the architectural principles governing consensus within CortexMesh.

Consensus is an evidence synthesis mechanism.

Consensus is not a voting mechanism.

Consensus is not authority.

---

# Principles

## CP-01

Consensus exists to improve evidence quality.

---

## CP-02

Independent reasoning precedes interaction.

---

## CP-03

Minority findings are preserved.

---

## CP-04

Disagreement is evidence.

---

## CP-05

Recommendations remain traceable to supporting evidence.

---

## CP-06

Human governance remains authoritative.

---

## CP-07

Consensus must remain reproducible and auditable.

---

## CP-08

Consensus shall never be represented as an aggregate intelligence score.
