## Reason

The Apache-2.0 licence did not align with the Product Owner’s commercial and intellectual-property strategy.

## Decision

CortexMesh will operate as proprietary software under an all-rights-reserved model. Collaboration will be limited to explicitly authorized parties.

## Actions

- Future Apache-2.0 distribution stopped.
- Repository access restricted.
- Apache licensing materials removed.
- Interim proprietary notice added.
- Open-community participation materials withdrawn.
- Formal legal review required before commercial licensing or third-party access arrangements are finalized.

## Historical Boundary

This correction does not claim to revoke rights already granted for copies legitimately obtained while Apache-2.0 applied.
