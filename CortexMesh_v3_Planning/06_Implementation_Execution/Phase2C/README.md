# Phase 2C - Intelligent Multi-Agent Orchestration

## Status

AUTHORIZED TO COMMENCE

## Objective

Commence intelligent orchestration engineering built upon the validated Local AI platform.

Phase 2C shifts focus from Local AI infrastructure to orchestration behavior, including capability-driven routing, multi-agent collaboration, evidence-aware reasoning, consensus support, and adaptive orchestration.

## Working Area

This directory is the active working area for Phase 2C records, status, evidence, risks, blockers, plans, and closeout material.

Phase 1B and Phase 2A are complete and immutable. Phase 2C uses its own evidence and milestone records.

## In Scope

- Capability-driven routing.
- Multi-agent collaboration.
- Evidence-aware reasoning.
- Consensus layer design and validation.
- Adaptive orchestration.
- Verification evidence.
- Documentation.

## Out of Scope

- Redesign of `LocalAIManager`.
- Redesign of `LocalAIProvider`.
- Provider-specific Local AI platform work.
- LOCKED component modification without future authorization.
- Scoring changes without future authorization.
- Authority changes without future authorization.
- Governance changes.

## Architectural Principles

1. The Local AI platform is stable and shall be treated as foundational infrastructure.
2. `LocalAIManager` and `LocalAIProvider` public contracts shall be preserved.
3. Provider neutrality shall be preserved.
4. Capabilities, evidence, and orchestration state shall drive engineering decisions.
5. GG-02 remains the active Board voting and ratification authority.
6. LOCKED component changes require future explicit authorization.

## Initial Workstreams

1. M1 - Capability-driven routing.
2. M2 - Multi-agent collaboration.
3. M3 - Evidence-aware reasoning.
4. M4 - Consensus layer.
5. M5 - Adaptive orchestration.

## Success Criteria

- Orchestration improvements are evidence-driven.
- Local AI platform contracts remain stable.
- Provider neutrality remains intact.
- No LOCKED boundaries are crossed without authorization.
- Verification evidence is preserved for every milestone.

## Recommendation

Proceed with Phase 2C under existing governance constraints and future authorization requirements for any LOCKED component modification.
