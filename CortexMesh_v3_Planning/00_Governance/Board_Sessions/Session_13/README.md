## Purpose

Session 13 requests an independent critical architectural, governance, engineering, and scientific review of CortexMesh Foundation 1.0 and Research Program RP-001.

No governance change, authority modification, runtime integration, implementation authorization, LOCKED modification, or Board vote is requested. The purpose is to identify architectural weaknesses, unsupported assumptions, research risks, overstatements, and future investigation opportunities.

## Review Boundary

Board members are asked to review and challenge the evidence, not ratify it. Responses are advisory critical-review records. They do not become Board decisions merely by being submitted, discussed, or summarized.

Any future proposal arising from this review must follow its own evidence, authorization, and GG-02 process.
