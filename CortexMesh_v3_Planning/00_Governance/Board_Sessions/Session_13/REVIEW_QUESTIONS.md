# Session 13 Review Questions

Reviewers are asked to answer every question independently and cite specific repository evidence.

## Q1

Did Foundation 1.0 achieve its published objectives?

## Q2

Identify the three greatest architectural weaknesses.

## Q3

Identify assumptions insufficiently supported by evidence.

## Q4

Do RP-001 conclusions remain proportional to the evidence?

## Q5

Are any conclusions overstated?

## Q6

Identify future research priorities.

## Q7

Should RP-002 proceed now? If not, what evidence is still missing?

## Q8

Would you recommend changes to the research methodology?

## Response Standard

For each answer:

- distinguish evidence from inference;
- identify missing or conflicting evidence;
- state severity and practical impact;
- preserve uncertainty;
- avoid provider/model identity as authority;
- do not convert a recommendation into authorization.
