# 3. Future Board Operating Model

To reduce unnecessary governance overhead while preserving constitutional integrity, future Board work will distinguish between two operating modes.

## Governance Board

Used only when constitutional authority is exercised.

Examples include:

* governance creation or amendment;
* LOCKED architecture decisions;
* Board Decisions;
* roadmap authorization;
* constitutional interpretation;
* formal ratification.

Governance Board mode continues to require compliance with GG-02.

---

## Expert Panel

Used for advisory and implementation activities including:

* engineering;
* software architecture;
* implementation planning;
* repository management;
* technical review;
* research;
* design validation;
* risk assessment.

Expert Panel activities are advisory and do not themselves exercise constitutional authority.

Each Expert Panel activity may designate a lead according to the expertise most appropriate for that work.

Examples include:

* **Lead: ChatGPT (Engineering Lead)**
* **Lead: DeepSeek (Critical Review Lead)**
* **Lead: Kimi (Governance Analysis Lead)**

These lead assignments coordinate work only.

They do not create additional governance authority or alter Board equality.
