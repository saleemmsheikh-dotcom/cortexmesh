# Document Authority Matrix

## Purpose

Defines which document is authoritative when governance documents conflict.

## Authority Order

| Priority | Document | Authority |

## Active Constitutional Authority

GG-02_Board_Voting_and_Ratification_Rules_v1.0.md is RATIFIED and IN FORCE as of 2026-07-06.

It controls Board quorum, voting, ratification, Product Owner endorsement requirements, and effective dates where those procedural rules conflict with other governance records.

### Board Voting Authority

GG-02_Board_Voting_and_Ratification_Rules_v1.0.md is the constitutional authority governing:

- Board quorum
- voting procedures
- voting thresholds
- abstentions
- ratification requirements
- effective dates
- Product Owner endorsement requirements for ratification

Where procedural rules governing Board voting or ratification conflict with other governance documents, GG-02_Board_Voting_and_Ratification_Rules_v1.0.md shall prevail.

All other governance matters continue to follow the authority order defined above unless a later unanimously ratified governance document expressly supersedes GG-02.

## Rule

If two documents conflict, the higher-priority document controls unless the board records a unanimous exception.

## Policy Override Rule

`BOARD_DECISIONS_REGISTER.md` overrides policy documents only when the board explicitly records that the decision:

- amends policy
- supersedes policy
- creates a policy exception

Otherwise the decision is interpreted as an application of existing policy.
