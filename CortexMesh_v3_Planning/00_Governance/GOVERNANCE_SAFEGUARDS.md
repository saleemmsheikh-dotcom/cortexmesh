# Governance Safeguards

**Status:** Ratified

**Source:** BD-09-003

---

## Purpose

These safeguards were adopted following the retrospective ratification of Session 09 LOCKED component modifications.

They form part of the CortexMesh governance baseline.

---

## S1 - LOCKED Components

Modification of any LOCKED component requires explicit unanimous Board authorization before implementation begins.

---

## S2 - External Agent Scope

External agents shall operate only within the scope explicitly authorized by the Board.

Audit authority does not imply implementation authority.

---

## S3 - Board Consultation

Consultation with an individual Board member shall never be interpreted as Board authorization.

Any proposal affecting governance or LOCKED components shall be escalated to the full Board before implementation.

---

## S4 - Governance Compliance Review

A periodic governance compliance review shall verify continued adherence to approved governance procedures and identify opportunities for improvement.

---

## S5 - External Implementation Authority

The Product Owner shall not commission external implementation work affecting LOCKED components without prior unanimous Board authorization.

---

## Governance Principle

These safeguards are preventative controls intended to preserve governance integrity and do not alter the Board's authority to approve retrospective ratification in exceptional circumstances.
