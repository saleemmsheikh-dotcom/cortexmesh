## Authority

The Governance Board unanimously ratified BD-12-003 by a vote of 3/3.
BD-12-003 authorizes Programme Alpha to proceed as a bounded Architecture Study
operating under advisory authority only.

The Product Owner authorized initiation of the Architecture Study effective
2026-07-23.

## Scope

The Architecture Study shall produce a bounded engineering blueprint addressing:

- architectural evolution principles;
- capability selection criteria;
- integration boundaries;
- transition pathways;
- measurable success criteria; and
- evidence thresholds for any future implementation proposal.

The Study shall explicitly evaluate, rather than presume:

- targeted Technical Debt Register enhancements;
- targeted SGC-001 refinements;
- roadmap evolution;
- a future strategic engineering programme; and
- no further action.

## Constraints

The Architecture Study:

- operates under advisory authority only;
- has no implementation authority;
- has no governance amendment authority;
- has no roadmap authorization authority;
- must preserve completed phases and historical governance records;
- must acknowledge all active Technical Debt items, TD-001 through TD-015;
- must not supersede or replace the Technical Debt Register; and
- must return any implementation proposal for separate unanimous Board approval
  under GG-02.

## Deliverables

The authorized deliverables are:

1. an Architecture Study report;
2. the bounded engineering blueprint defined by the approved scope;
3. supporting evidence and findings;
4. recommendations for Board consideration; and
5. explicit identification of any proposed implementation requiring a separate
   GG-02 decision.

## Reporting Requirements

The Study Lead shall:

- preserve the evidence supporting findings and recommendations;
- distinguish advisory findings from any future implementation proposal;
- report the completed Study, evidence, and recommendations to the Governance
  Board;
- identify unresolved issues and limitations; and
- request no implementation action without a separate unanimous Board decision
  under GG-02.
