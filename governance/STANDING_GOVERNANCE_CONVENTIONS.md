# Standing Governance Conventions

Status:
ACTIVE REGISTRY

---

# Purpose

Maintain the registry of Standing Governance Conventions adopted or proposed for CortexMesh Board operational practice.

Standing Governance Conventions define recurring governance operations and discussion-management practices.

They do not alter governance authority, Board responsibilities, Board voting, architectural governance, implementation authority, or policy interpretation unless separately approved through recorded Board decision.

---

# Convention Lifecycle

Standing Governance Conventions may use the following statuses:

- PROPOSED
- RATIFIED
- AMENDED
- REPEALED

A convention marked PROPOSED carries no governance authority until ratified by recorded Board decision.

A convention marked RATIFIED applies to CortexMesh sessions according to its stated scope until amended or repealed by recorded Board decision.

---

# SGC-001 - Board Session Discussion Management

Status:
RATIFIED

Category:
Operational Governance

---

## Title

Board Session Discussion Management

---

## Convention

The CortexMesh Board shall normally conduct governance deliberations using one dedicated discussion thread for each project session.

Each project session shall be treated as an independent governance discussion record.

At the commencement of every new project session:

- a new Board discussion thread shall be created;
- the previous session discussion shall be considered closed unless explicitly reopened by recorded Board decision;
- the new discussion shall begin with a Session Baseline summarizing:
  - current project phase;
  - previous session outcomes;
  - current governance status;
  - current objectives;
  - current deliverables;
  - active governance constraints.

The Session Baseline shall reference previously ratified work rather than reproducing historical discussions.

Cross-session discussion should normally be avoided except when historical governance decisions require clarification or when a previous session is formally reopened by Board decision.

---

## Purpose

This convention exists to:

- improve governance traceability;
- preserve clear audit boundaries;
- reduce discussion overhead;
- simplify Board review;
- maintain one authoritative governance discussion per project session.

---

## Scope

This convention governs Board operational practice only.

It does not modify:

- governance authority;
- Board responsibilities;
- Board voting;
- Product Owner responsibilities;
- policy interpretation;
- architectural decisions;
- architectural governance;
- implementation authority.

---

## Applicability

This convention is session-neutral.

If ratified, it applies to all future CortexMesh sessions unless amended or repealed by recorded Board decision.
