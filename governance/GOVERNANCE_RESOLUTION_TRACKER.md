# Execution Rule

Implementation Planning beyond IMP002 shall remain paused until all governance blockers have completed policy implementation and verification following recorded Board decision.

Non-blocking findings shall either:

- be resolved,
- be deferred by recorded Board decision, or
- be accepted into the governance record.

# Completion Rule

Governance Resolution is complete when:

- all blockers are closed,
- all non-blocking findings have recorded dispositions,
- the Board records Governance Review Complete,
- Session 10 execution resumes.
